This is a list of the biggest cities in Serbia. By far the largest agglomeration in Serbia is Belgrade with one Population of 1,755,625 (as of January 1, 2006). 16 percent of them live with that People of the country in the capital region. The following table contains the cities over 20,000 inhabitants, the results of the censuses of March 31, 1991 and April 1, 2002 as well as a calculation for January 1, 2006. The higher-level administrative unit is also listed (Province of Kosovo and Metohija and Vojvodina) to which the city belongs. That part of Serbia (more than half of the country) that is not among them belongs to both provinces, does not form a separate political unit, therefore there is no official name for him either. The name is informal Central Serbia in use. The population figures refer to the respective urban settlement in the narrower sense - the geographical one Urban area - not the city or municipality in a political sense. These are given in the list of municipalities in Serbia.
